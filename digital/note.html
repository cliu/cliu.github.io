Notes On The Wall

Oct. 2018

This is a rapid production challenge. We need to make a short film in 48 hours from scratch.
In this film, the character woke up in his car, and found a note in the window. What he doesn't know is, the person who wrote the note is closer than he thinks.

Team Size: 5
Teammates: Jue Wang, Swapnil Mengade, Chenchen (Ava) Tan, Kristian Tchetchko
Duration: 48 hours
My Role: Cameraman & Editor
