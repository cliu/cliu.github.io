Zombie Escape

Sep. 2018

In this game, the guest will be put into a dark place with three torches, with the goal to find a blue door and escape. The guest can use the torch to light the environment for a while, and collect torches along the way. It has three levels, level 3 is super hard.

Team Size: 1
Duration: 1 week
My Role: Game programmer
